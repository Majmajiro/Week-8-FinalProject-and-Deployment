About Class Measures Limited

Founded with a passion for innovative education, Class Measures Limited is dedicated to helping children develop essential life skills through engaging and fun educational programs.

Our unique approach combines traditional learning with modern technology, creating an environment where children can explore, experiment, and excel. We believe that by teaching through play and practical activities, children develop a genuine love for learning!

Our Mission

We are dedicated to nurturing confident, creative, and curious learners by integrating play and technology into afterschool education.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow in a supportive environment.

Our mission is to provide engaging programs that foster a love for learning and equip children with the skills they need to thrive in the 21st century.
