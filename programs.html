💻 Coding

We introduce kids to the world of programming using Scratch, Code.org, and fun web-based tools. They build games, animations, and logical thinking!

♟️ Chess

Through strategy and focus, children learn how to think ahead, stay patient, and sharpen their minds in a fun and competitive way.



🤖 Robotics

Kids explore the basics of robotics and engineering using hands-on kits, sensors, and logic to build and control cool creations.

🗼 French

We make learning French interactive and fun with songs, stories, and real-world vocabulary to spark a love for languages.

📚 Reading

Our reading program uses digital platforms like Scholastic Learning Zone to help children build fluency, vocabulary, and comprehension.

🌟 Benefits Beyond the Classroom

Our programs don't just teach technical skills—they develop essential life skills that benefit children in all areas of their lives.

  • 🧠 Critical Thinking: Analyze situations, evaluate options, and make informed decisions.
  • 🎨 Creativity: Encourage innovation and unique problem-solving approaches.
  • 🤝 Collaboration: Develop teamwork and communication skills.
  • 💪 Confidence: Build self-assurance through skill mastery.
  • 🔥 Resilience: Learn to embrace challenges and persevere.
  • 💻 Digital Literacy: Gain essential tech skills for today’s world.

Join us in nurturing the next generation of innovators and leaders!
